The Best Movies According to Shamim

My top 3 movies of all-time

Interstellar

Interstellar is a 2014 epic science fiction film co-written, directed, and produced by Christopher Nolan. This is my most favourite moive that time.

Taare Zameen Par

Taare Zameen Par , also known as Like Stars on Earth in English, is a 2007 Indian Hindi-language drama film produced and directed by Aamir Khan. Best Hindi Movie I ever seen.

Pather Panchali

Pather Panchali (Song of the Little Road) is a 1955 Indian Bengali-language drama film written and directed by Satyajit Ray in his directoral debut and produced by the Government of West Bengal. It was among the films that pioneered the Parallel cinema movement, which espoused authenticity and social realism.
